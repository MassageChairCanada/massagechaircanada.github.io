FAQ

Is a massage chair worth it in Canada?

If you’ll use it multiple times per week, a premium chair can replace repeated massage appointments over time. Value comes from fit, comfort, and consistency, not just “more programs”.

What is the best massage chair for back pain?

Many people use massage chairs to reduce muscle tension and improve relaxation. For back-focused comfort, prioritize adjustable intensity, accurate body scanning, and long track coverage (SL-track).

Is a 4D massage chair better than 3D?

3D changes roller depth. 4D adds speed and rhythm variation, which often feels more natural and easier to tune for different users. For a premium home experience, 4D is usually the upgrade.

What should I look for when buying a massage chair for home?

Measure your space, confirm recline clearance, and prioritize track length, intensity control, and comfort features you’ll use daily (zero gravity and heat).
