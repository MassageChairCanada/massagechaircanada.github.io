FAQ

Where should I start?

Start with Best Massage Chair (Canada) for the current #1 pick and short list.

What should I measure before buying?

Doorway width, hallway turns, and recline clearance. Confirm whether delivery includes in-home placement.

Do I need 4D?

4D can feel more natural, but it’s a big price jump. For most homes, daily comfort + heat + easy use beats “max tech”.
